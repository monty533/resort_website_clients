OUR ROOMS

The Red Leaf Resort And Cafe has 80 beautifully appointed guestrooms, adorned with boutique furnishings and warm earth tones that give each space a stylish, contemporary feel. This is complemented by the grand red brick architecture with high ceilings and relaxing vintage atmosphere throughout. A calm resort with ample light and ventilation for maximum comfort.

The resort also has specially designed rooms for guests with special needs. The property team has developed the infrastructure for physically challenged, including specially equipped guestrooms, paths for the blind, and other public areas and facilities.
