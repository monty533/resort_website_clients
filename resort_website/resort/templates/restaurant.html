DINING

The Piccadilly restaurant can be located on the first floor of the hotel and enjoys a reputation for superb fresh food served by attentive staff.

It offers an ideal variety of Western cuisine like tandoori grill steaks. The warm and friendly restaurant staff are here to provide you with the best level of service and will ensure that your dining experience will be a memorable one.

The Executive Chef along with his culinary team is constantly creating new and innovative cuisines, including more than a dozen vegetarian options and organic farm-fresh dishes from the hotel.

On request we can arrange for a farm tour to our organic garden.
